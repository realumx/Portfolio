About

A developer who likes solving real problems, not just writing code.

I'm Umesh Kumar, a web developer focused on the MERN stack — MongoDB, Express, React, and Node.js. Over the past couple of years I've built full-stack applications that range from small utility tools to multi-page platforms with authentication, dashboards, and REST APIs.
